About Us

Melody Makers is a vibrant music organization dedicated to fostering musical talent and creating unforgettable experiences. We bring together musicians, artists, and music enthusiasts in a celebration of creativity and harmony.

Our Founders

Prasanna Batala

Founder & Music Director

A prodigious talent who began his musical journey at a young age, Prasanna has mastered both vocal performance and multiple musical instruments. His dedication to music and natural talent have made him a respected figure in the music community. His passion for teaching and sharing musical knowledge has inspired countless aspiring musicians.

Hitesh Bikram Shah

Co-Founder & Creative Director

A visionary in the music industry, Hitesh brings innovative ideas and creative direction to Melody Makers. His expertise in music production and event management has been instrumental in creating memorable musical experiences. His leadership and dedication have helped shape the organization into a thriving community for music enthusiasts.
